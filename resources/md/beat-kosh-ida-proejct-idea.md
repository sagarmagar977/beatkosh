```text
Introduction 
► Hybrid Marketplace: A unified platform for freelance music production 
and a digital beat catalog.
► Two-Way Collaboration: Artists can instantly buy ready-made beats or 
hire producers for full album production.
► Nepali Focus: Specifically designed to bridge the gap in the local music 
industry using local payment and culture.
```

## Page 4

```text
Problem Statement 
► Europe has “ BeatStars “ , India has “ Beat22 “ and Nepal has no such platforms.
► Unprotected Beats: Nepali producers often share work on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, leading to unauthorized use and piracy.
► Payment Barriers: Global platforms like BeatStars don't easily integrate with local payment methods.
► Disorganized Freelancing: Currently, hiring a producer in Nepal involves informal, unprotected deals via social media.
► Lack of Centralized Catalog: No professional platform exists where artists can browse high-quality local instrumentals for their singles or albums.
► No "Full-Album" Service: Current platforms focus on single beats, not the long-term collaboration needed for cohesive album production.
```

## Page 5

```text
Motivation 
► Supporting Local Talent: Helping music producers/beatmakers in Nepal 
earn from their art.
► Industry Growth: Aligning with the rise of Nepali Hip-Hop (Nephop) and 
inde-studio music.
► Digital Sovereignty: Providing a "Made in Nepal" alternative to foreign 
marketplaces.
```

## Page 6

```text
Objective 
► To build a Multi-Vendor system where producers manage their own 
freelance profiles and beat stores.
► To implement a Hiring Module for milestone-based album production
► To integrate Local Payment Gateways (eSewa, Khalti, ConnectIPS) for 
accessibility.
► To implement Audio Fingerprinting (Dejavu/Python) for copyright 
protection.
```

## Page 7

```text
Features 
► Producer Catalog: Instant-purchase instrumentals with automated 
licensing.
► Freelance Hire Option: Milestone-based system for producing full 
albums or custom singles.
► Smart Discovery: An intelligent recommendation engine that analyzes 
past purchases and listening habits to suggest the perfect instrumentals 
or beats that significantly boosts conversion rates and sales.
► Advanced Analytics: Dashboard for producers to track  Sales analytics, 
play counts, and "skip-rate" charts. and hiring requests.
```
